# Byblos Digital Product User Guide

Welcome to Byblos! Our digital products are protected by the **Byblos Armored (.bybx)** system to ensure that creators are fairly compensated for their work.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### What is a .bybx file?
A `.bybx` file is a secure container for digital content (like PDFs or Audio). It ensures that only the person who purchased the product can access it.

### How do I open my purchase?
1. **Download** the `.bybx` file from your Order History.
2. **Open** the file in your browser (Chrome or Edge recommended).
3. The platform will automatically verify your license and unlock the content.

### Can I access my files on multiple devices?
Currently, your purchase is bound to the **first device** you use to open it. If you need to access it on a new computer, please contact support to reset your device binding.
*(Note: We are working on supporting up to 3 devices simultaneously soon!)*

### Can I read my files offline?
At this time, an internet connection is required to verify your license each time you open the file. We recommend keeping the tab open if you plan to go offline.

### What browsers are supported?
For the best experience, use:
- **Google Chrome** (Desktop)
- **Microsoft Edge** (Desktop)
- **Brave Browser** (Desktop)

*Note: Mobile browsers and Safari/Firefox currently have limited support for opening .bybx files directly.*

## Troubleshooting Guide

### "Hardware Mismatch" Error
This happens if you try to open the file on a different computer than the one you used for the first time. 
- **Solution**: Open the file on your original device, or contact support for a device transfer.

### "Not a valid Byblos Armored file"
The file may have been corrupted during download.
- **Solution**: Go to your Order History and download a fresh copy of the file.

### Nothing happens when I open the file
Your browser might be blocking the file handler.
- **Solution**: Ensure you are using a supported browser (Chrome/Edge) and that you haven't disabled the "File Handling" permission for bybloshq.com.
